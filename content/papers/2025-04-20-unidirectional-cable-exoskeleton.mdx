## 中文摘要

本文设计了一套便携式绳索驱动并联外骨骼，集成肘关节、腕关节和手指的康复功能，共 7 个自由度。驱动器置于背包中（1.2–1.5 kg），通过绳索传动实现远端驱动。在 8 名健康受试者上的实验表明，外骨骼辅助下主动肌激活**平均降低 21.3%**。该研究验证了绳索驱动外骨骼在减少肌肉负担方面的有效性，为临床应用奠定了基础。

## English Summary

This paper presents a portable cable-driven parallel exoskeleton integrating elbow, wrist, and finger assistance across 7 DOF, with actuators housed in a backpack (1.2–1.5 kg). Experiments with 8 healthy participants showed an average 21.3% reduction in agonist muscle activation with exoskeleton assistance. The study validates the effectiveness of cable-driven exoskeletons in reducing muscular effort, laying groundwork for clinical application.
