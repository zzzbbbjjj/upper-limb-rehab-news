## 中文摘要

本文设计了一款面向家庭和临床环境的便携式手-腕康复机器人。核心创新包括：新型四杆机构结合 Bowden 线缆手指传动系统，支持被动、等长和主动三种训练模式，覆盖屈曲/伸展和桡偏/尺偏运动。采用轻量化电动执行器驱动，已在健康受试者上完成验证，具备良好的临床转化前景。该系统体现了绳索驱动康复机器人向**便携化和家用化**发展的趋势。

## English Summary

This paper presents a versatile, portable rehabilitation robot for both home and clinical environments. Key innovations include a novel four-bar mechanism with Bowden cable finger transmission, supporting passive, isometric, and active exercises for flexion/extension and radial/ulnar deviation. Validated on healthy subjects with lightweight electrical actuators, the system is ready for patient trials, exemplifying the trend toward portable and home-based cable-driven rehabilitation.
