## 中文摘要

清华大学唐晓强团队提出了一种**3自由度索驱动腕康复机器人（CDWRR）**，采用4根绳索实现腕关节背伸/掌屈、尺偏/桡偏和旋内/旋外三个自由度的康复训练。力控工作空间分析表明，系统满足桡偏/尺偏全范围需求、掌屈/背伸87.7%和旋外/旋内38.7%的ADL运动需求。灵巧性指标范围为1.6~4.0，表明运动灵巧性良好。特别提出了结合**导纳控制的被动柔顺康复训练策略**，当人机交互力矩超过安全阈值时机器人顺应运动，有效避免二次损伤。

## English Summary

The Tang team at Tsinghua University developed a **3-DOF Cable-Driven Wrist Rehabilitation Robot (CDWRR)** using 4 cables for wrist flexion/extension, radial/ulnar deviation, and pronation/supination. The wrench-feasible workspace covers 100% of radial/ulnar deviation, 87.7% of flexion/extension, and 38.7% of pronation/supination ADL demands. The dexterity index (condition number) ranges 1.6–4.0, indicating good manipulability. A **passive compliance training strategy with admittance control** was proposed — when human-robot interaction torque exceeds safety thresholds, the robot complies to prevent secondary injury.
