## 中文摘要

针对**脑卒中后手指挛缩期**患者肌肉无力导致手指无法伸展的临床需求，本文提出了一种绳索与板簧复合驱动方案：**高刚度板簧**辅助手指伸展，**绳索**完成屈曲和抓握。性能评估图面积覆盖率达理想外骨骼的70.4%，假手实验验证了多种手势和抓握训练的有效性。复合驱动策略充分利用了绳索在屈曲方向的力优势和弹性元件在伸展方向的储能特性。

## English Summary

Targeting **post-stroke finger contracture** patients where muscle weakness prevents finger extension, this paper proposes a compound drive combining **high-stiffness leaf springs** for extension assistance and **cables** for flexion/grasping. Performance evaluation achieved 70.4% coverage of the ideal exoskeleton workspace. Prosthetic hand experiments validated effectiveness across multiple gestures and grip patterns. The compound strategy exploits the strength of cables in flexion and the energy-storage property of elastic elements in extension.
