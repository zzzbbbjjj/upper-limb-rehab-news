## 中文摘要

CureHand 外骨骼采用绳索-连杆混合机械结构，专为**手部痉挛的定量评估**而设计。该装置可精确测量手指关节位置与阻力力矩，结合生物力学与神经学指标建立多层评估体系。在6名参与者上完成了主要手指关节的验证实验。该研究将绳索驱动外骨骼的应用从单纯康复训练拓展到**临床定量评估**领域，为个性化康复方案制定提供了客观数据支撑。

## English Summary

The CureHand exoskeleton uses a cable-linkage mechanical structure specifically designed for **quantitative hand spasticity assessment**. It enables precise measurement of finger joint positions and resistive forces, using a multi-layered assessment approach with biomechanical and neurological metrics. Validated on six participants across primary finger joints, this work extends cable-driven exoskeletons beyond rehabilitation training into **clinical quantitative assessment**, providing objective data for personalized treatment planning.
