## 中文摘要

锡耶纳大学团队提出了基于**扭转绳驱动器（Twisted String Actuators, TSA）** 的模块化肌腱驱动康复手套。TSA通过绳索扭转缩短产生线性运动，具有极高的力传递比和小惯量优势。手套可同时驱动拇指和食指的屈曲/伸展及内收/外展。基于 MATLAB Syngrasp 工具箱建立了手部生物力学参数化模型，并设计了可定制的绳索路径系统。模块化设计使系统可针对不同手指和不同患者需求灵活配置。

## English Summary

The University of Siena team developed a modular tendon-driven rehabilitation glove based on **Twisted String Actuators (TSA)**, which convert motor rotation into linear motion with high force transmission ratio and low inertia. The glove simultaneously drives thumb and index finger flexion/extension and abduction/adduction. A parametric hand biomechanics model was built using the MATLAB Syngrasp toolbox, with customizable cable routing paths. The modular design enables flexible configuration for different fingers and patient needs.
