## 中文摘要

本文提出一种新型可穿戴手部康复机器人，解决了绳索驱动外骨骼中的两大挑战：佩戴舒适性与力传递效率。创新点包括：（1）**可调刚柔混合锚环**——将硅胶材料与3D打印刚性树脂结合，通过可调旋钮机构适应不同手指尺寸；（2）**磁流变绳索驱动机构**——实现四种功能模式（协调运动、自适应抓握、独立手指控制、机械安全）的无缝切换。该系统已在脑卒中患者上完成抓握辅助和日常生活任务验证，展现了良好的临床应用前景。

## English Summary

This paper proposes a novel wearable hand robot that addresses two major challenges in cable-driven exoskeletons: comfort and force transmission efficiency. Key innovations include: (1) **Adjustable Rigid-Soft Anchor Rings** combining silicone comfort with 3D-printed resin rigidity, featuring an adjustable knob mechanism; (2) **Magnetorheological Cable-Driven Mechanism** enabling four-mode switching between coordinated motion, adaptive grasping, independent finger control, and mechanical safety. The system was validated on stroke patients for grasping assistance and ADL tasks, demonstrating strong clinical potential.
