## 中文摘要

受维格纳将基本粒子分类为时空对称群的不可约酉表示的启发，我们询问什么群可以以这种方式产生披着红外规范玻色子云的粒子的量子态。我们证明答案是由标准渐近对称性（例如万有引力情况下的 BMS）给出的，并由 arXiv:2305.05436 中确定的对数对称性进行补充。一个推论是将修饰粒子的希尔伯特空间分解为“裸”单粒子态空间与软规范玻色子希尔伯特空间的张量积。如果没有对数变换就无法获得后者，这最终导致了关键的海森堡中心扩展的存在。

## English Summary

Inspired by Wigner's classification of elementary particles as irreducible unitary representations of a spacetime symmetry group, we ask what group can give rise in this way to the quantum states of a particle dressed with clouds of infrared gauge bosons. We show that the answer is given by standard asymptotic symmetries (such as BMS in the gravitational case), supplemented by the logarithmic symmetries identified in arXiv:2305.05436. A corollary is the factorization of the Hilbert space of a dressed particle as a tensor product of the space of `naked' one-particle states with a Hilbert space of soft gauge bosons. The latter cannot be obtained without logarithmic transformations, which are ultimately responsible for the presence of a crucial Heisenberg central extension.
