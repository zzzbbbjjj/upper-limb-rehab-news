## 中文摘要

本论文开发了一种软绳索驱动手部外骨骼，实现了**无需触觉传感器的力估计**方法。系统集成三种感知模态：**EMG 肌电信号**用于用户意图检测、**计算机视觉**用于物体识别、**模型驱动力估计**替代传统触觉传感器。采用 Bowden 线缆传输和 3D 打印交叉指形肌腱结构，实测手指力 8.33–11.72 N。多模态以用户为中心的控制策略可在抓握、保持和释放模式间自动切换，目标用户为脑卒中幸存者。

## English Summary

This thesis develops a soft cable-driven hand exoskeleton with model-based force estimation — eliminating the need for tactile sensors. The system integrates three sensing modalities: EMG for user intent detection, computer vision for object recognition, and model-based force estimation replacing tactile sensors. Features Bowden cable transmission with 3D-printed crisscross finger tendons. Measured finger forces of 8.33–11.72 N. Multimodal user-centered control automatically switches between grasping, holding, and releasing modes targeting stroke survivors.
