## 中文摘要

本文介绍了一款专为脑卒中康复设计的绳索驱动手部外骨骼，仅需**两个电机**驱动四指（拇指独立），手部组件仅重 **246 克**。绳索机构避开手掌侧，使患者可直接接触真实物体。通过 ANOVA 分析（F = 57.2, p < 0.001）验证了高重复性，56 秒内完成穿戴。健康受试者测试无疲劳或不适报告，已获准进入脑卒中患者临床试验阶段。该研究为绳索驱动外骨骼从实验室走向临床提供了重要的**安全性和可靠性数据**。

## English Summary

This paper presents a cable-driven hand exoskeleton for stroke rehabilitation that actuates all four fingers together with only two motors, weighing just 246 g on the hand. The cable mechanism avoids the palmar side, allowing interaction with real objects. High repeatability was confirmed via ANOVA (F = 57.2, p < 0.001), with setup time of ~56 seconds. No fatigue or discomfort was reported by healthy participants. Cleared for clinical trials with stroke subjects, this study provides critical safety and reliability data for cable-driven exoskeletons.
