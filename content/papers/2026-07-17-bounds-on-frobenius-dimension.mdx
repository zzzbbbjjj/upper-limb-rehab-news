## 中文摘要

在本文中，我们证明了有关关联代数 Frobenius 维数的两种结果。首先，我们根据向量空间的维数细化有限维代数的 Frobenius 维数的已知上限，并表明达到该界限的唯一代数是与单顶点颤动相关的根式平方零代数。其次，我们明确计算低维代数的弗罗贝尼乌斯维数，并根据其基础箭袋中没有迂回的路径计算截断路径代数。

## English Summary

In this article, we prove two types of results about the Frobenius dimension of associative algebras. First, we refine the known upper bound for the Frobenius dimension of a finite dimensional algebra in terms of its dimension as a vector space, and show that the only algebras reaching this bound are radical square zero algebras associated with single-vertex quivers. Second, we compute Frobenius dimension for low-dimensional algebras explicitly, and for truncated path algebras in terms of paths with no detours in their underlying quivers.
