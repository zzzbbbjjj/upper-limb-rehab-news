## 中文摘要

本文设计了一款 3 自由度绳索驱动上肢外骨骼，覆盖肩关节外展/内收、肩关节屈曲/伸展和肘关节屈曲/伸展。系统总重约 1.7 kg，实现了 PID 位置控制和阻抗控制两种策略。在 5 名健康受试者上的测试表明，该系统可提供可控的被动运动辅助，关节轨迹跟踪精度满足康复训练要求。该研究为绳索驱动在**多关节上肢康复**中的应用提供了工程参考。

## English Summary

This paper presents a 3-DOF cable-driven upper-extremity exoskeleton covering shoulder abduction/adduction, shoulder flexion/extension, and elbow flexion/extension. The system weighs ~1.7 kg and implements both PID position control and impedance control. Testing with 5 healthy subjects demonstrated feasible passive motion assistance with controlled joint trajectories, providing an engineering reference for multi-joint upper limb cable-driven rehabilitation.
