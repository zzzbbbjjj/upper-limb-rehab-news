## 中文摘要

SRIG-Exo 是一款轻量、可穿戴的**意图控制欠驱动绳索驱动软体机器人手套**。采用可拆卸线缆连接器和线性驱动器（丝杠螺母机构），集成触觉传感器、弯曲传感器和 EMG 意图检测，实现主动辅助控制。最大线缆位移 10 cm，抓握力达 30 N。系统专为手部肌力减弱人群设计，可辅助完成进食、饮水等日常生活活动。该研究代表了绳索驱动康复从被动训练向**主动意图感知辅助**的重要演进。

## English Summary

SRIG-Exo is a lightweight, wearable, underactuated cable-driven soft robotic glove with a detachable cable connector and linear actuator mechanism. It integrates tactile sensors, flex sensors, and EMG-based intent detection for active-controlled assistance. Maximum cable displacement of 10 cm with grasping force up to 30 N. Designed for individuals with weakened hand strength to perform ADLs such as eating and drinking, representing the evolution from passive training to active intention-sensing assistance.
