## 中文摘要

我们研究图完美匹配的翻转动力学，其中翻转包括用互补的交替边替换沿偶循环的完美匹配的边。特别是，我们希望限制循环的最小长度，以便任何两个完美匹配都通过此类循环的翻转来关联。   给定一个有限图 $G$，我们考虑通过用满足其子图中存在完美匹配的适当条件的装饰替换 $G$ 的每个顶点而获得的装饰图族。我们证明了这个家族的一般上限。然后，我们获得了两个装饰族的更强界限：派系装饰和费舍尔装饰，后者假设底层图是平面的。在这两种情况下，边界都与装饰的大小无关。

## English Summary

We study the flip dynamics on perfect matchings of graphs, where a flip consists of replacing the edges of a perfect matching along an even cycle with the complementary alternating edges. In particular, we want to bound the minimum length of cycles such that any two perfect matchings are related by flips of such cycles.   Given a finite graph $G$, we consider the families of decorated graphs obtained by replacing each vertex of $G$ with a decoration satisfying suitable conditions on the existence of perfect matchings in its subgraphs. We prove a general upper bound for this family. We then obtain stronger bounds for two families of decorations: clique decorations and Fisher decorations, the latter under the assumption that the underlying graph is planar. In both cases, the bounds are independent of the sizes of the decorations.
