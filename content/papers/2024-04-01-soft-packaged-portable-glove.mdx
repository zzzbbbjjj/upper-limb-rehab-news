## 中文摘要

发表于 **Nature Machine Intelligence** 的重磅研究，提出了一种软体封装便携式康复手套，能实现**闭环精细运动技能训练**。该手套采用创新的软体封装技术将驱动、传感和控制集成于一体，突破了传统康复设备笨重、仅能进行粗大运动训练的局限。通过闭环控制策略，手套可以实时感知手指位置和力，实现精准的抓握力调节。该研究将绳索/软体驱动的手部康复推向了**顶级学术期刊**的关注焦点。

## English Summary

A landmark study published in **Nature Machine Intelligence** presenting a soft-packaged portable rehabilitation glove capable of closed-loop fine motor skills training. The glove integrates actuation, sensing, and control through innovative soft packaging technology, overcoming the limitations of bulky traditional devices restricted to gross motor training. Closed-loop control enables real-time finger position and force sensing for precise grip force modulation. This work elevated cable/soft-driven hand rehabilitation to the attention of top-tier academic journals.
