## 中文摘要

本文开发了一款集成 sEMG 传感的轻量级软体康复手套，**手套本体仅重 50 克**。系统采用欠驱动绳索机构，将 sEMG 电极直接集成到手套中，实现比例控制方法，并结合**镜像治疗**策略进行康复训练。最大抓握力达 35 N，可无需主动控制自适应抓握多种形状物体。该研究为低成本、轻量化的家用绳索驱动康复手套提供了重要设计参考。

## English Summary

This paper developed a lightweight soft robotic glove with integrated sEMG sensing, weighing only 50 g. The system uses an underactuated cable mechanism with sEMG electrodes embedded in the glove, implementing proportional control combined with Mirror Therapy for rehabilitation. Maximum grasp force of 35 N, capable of handling objects of various shapes without active control. This work provides important design reference for low-cost, lightweight home-use cable-driven rehabilitation gloves.
