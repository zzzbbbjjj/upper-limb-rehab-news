## 中文摘要

本文设计了一款采用 **Bowden 线缆机构**的可穿戴绳索驱动肩部外骨骼服（CDSE），用于多自由度上肢辅助。系统总重约 2 kg，最大辅助负载 4 kg。与传统刚性外骨骼不同，CDSE 采用柔性纺织基底配合绳索传动，大幅提升了穿戴舒适性。该研究代表了绳索驱动从手部向**整个上肢链条**（肩-肘-腕-手）扩展的趋势，为全上肢协调康复提供了技术基础。

## English Summary

This paper presents a wearable cable-driven shoulder exosuit (CDSE) using Bowden cable mechanisms for multi-DOF upper limb assistance. The system weighs ~2 kg and supports loads up to 4 kg. Unlike traditional rigid exoskeletons, CDSE uses a soft textile base with cable transmission, significantly improving wearing comfort. This work represents the expansion of cable-driven rehabilitation from the hand to the entire upper limb chain (shoulder-elbow-wrist-hand), providing a technical foundation for coordinated full-arm rehabilitation.
