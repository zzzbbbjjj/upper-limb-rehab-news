## 中文摘要

本研究首次探索了**绳索驱动并联机器人（CDPR）** 在腕关节和手指一体化康复中的应用。通过建立腕部和手指的运动学模型，采用逆运动学方法进行轨迹规划。原型样机验证表明，该系统在精度和运动范围方面均达到康复训练需求。CDPR 的引入为多关节协调康复提供了全新思路——用一个系统同时服务手腕和手指，避免了传统外骨骼需要多个独立驱动器的问题。

## English Summary

This study explores **Cable-Driven Parallel Robots (CDPRs)** for combined wrist and finger rehabilitation. Kinematic models for both wrist and fingers were developed using an inverse kinematic approach. The prototype demonstrated satisfactory accuracy and range of motion, paving the way for multi-joint coordinated rehabilitation with a single system. The CDPR approach eliminates the need for multiple independent actuators typically required in traditional exoskeletons.
