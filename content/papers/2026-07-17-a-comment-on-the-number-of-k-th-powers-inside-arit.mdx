## 中文摘要

在 \cite{BD} 中，Bourgain 和 Demeter 发现了步长有 $O(1)$ 个除数的任意算术级数中 $k$ 次方的数量的尖锐上限。我们很容易观察到，如果步长相对于级数的长度增长得不是太快，那么相同的论点仍然有效。此外，对于足够大的 $N$，我们对第一个 $N$ 项中的 $k$ 次幂的数量给出了尖锐的界限。这两个结果都应该是已知的。尽管如此，我们还是对文献进行了补充。

## English Summary

In \cite{BD} Bourgain and Demeter found sharp upper bounds for the number of $k$-th powers inside arbitrary arithmetic progressions whose step has $O(1)$ many divisors. We make the easy observation that the same arguments are still valid if the step does not grow too rapidly in relation to the length of the progression. Furthermore, we give sharp bounds for the number of $k$-th powers among the first $N$ terms for $N$ large enough. Both results should be known. Nevertheless, we add to the literature.
