## 中文摘要

本篇范围综述分析了**135 篇**关于手部和腕部主动驱动外骨骼的研究论文。核心发现：在技术就绪度 TRL 3（概念验证）级别的设备中，**47% 采用了绳索管路机构**（含 Bowden 线缆）。这些设备主要用于手部支持，平均 2–3 个自由度。该综述全面梳理了从刚性连杆到软体绳索的驱动技术演进路线，是了解该领域整体格局的必读文献。

## English Summary

This scoping review analyzed **135 research papers** on active, actuated assistive devices for hands and wrists. Key finding: 47% of TRL 3 (proof-of-concept) devices used cable conduit mechanisms (including Bowden cables), primarily for hand support with 2–3 DOF on average. The review comprehensively maps the evolution from rigid linkages to soft cable-driven actuation, making it essential reading for understanding the field's landscape.
